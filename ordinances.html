Lemoyne State Ordinances

Take it out of town

A City is not a warzone. If you have beef then take it out of town limits. If Gangs and families are found to commit acts of violence within town limits they will be met with zero tolerance and no reductions upon arrest. And you WILL lose all of your firearms.

Dismemberment and Abuse of a Corpse

Individuals witnessed walking up to an injured or otherwise subdued person whether civilian, outlaw, or lawman and delivers a fatal shot to the head or removes limbs by gunfire or other means of violence will be charged with decapitation with intent.

Penalty: $200 Fine + 10 Months in a correctional facility.

Impersonation of Official Personnel

Impersonation of a Lawman, Physician, Court Officer, or Government official will no longer result in a warning. It will be an immediate arrest.

Penalty: $500 Fine + 20 Months in a Correctional Facility + Confiscation of any documents and outfits used.

Oil Wagons in Town

Oil Wagons are to be used only for their intended purpose and must not be brought into towns unless delivering oil during a mission. Misuse of an Oil Wagon (using it as a weapon) will result in a charge of Arson and all weapons shall be removed from the offender and those with them.

Lasso Ordinance

A lasso is not a toy. Use of a lasso to tie an individual up whether it be goofing about or serious intent or just to trip them up will result in confiscation of the lasso and a brandishing charge. This does not apply to proper use of a lasso (e.g., fleeing horses, use by medical professionals to stitch wounds).

Rooftop Occupation Without Cause

Anyone found on rooftops in St. Denis, Rhodes, and Van Horn without a good reason will receive a Loitering charge.

Business Protection Act

Any Person who disturbs or commits unlawful acts inside of a business (i.e. Tailor Shops, Gunsmiths, Stables, etc.) including fraud or scamming inside the business shall be subject to the following penalties:

  • First Offense: Loitering Charge (10 Months prison time + $250 Fine)
  • Second Offense: $450.00 Fine
